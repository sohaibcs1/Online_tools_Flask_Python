What Is IP Address?

An IP address is a numerical identification that is assigned to each device. It specifies the device's location in a network as well as a path to get there. An IP address is used by the internet to send IP packets from a source to a destination.

Can Someone Find Me With My IP Address?

It does not disclose your location. If someone obtains your IP address, they can discover a little bit about your internet service, such as which provider you use to connect to the internet, but they cannot locate you, your home, or your business.
