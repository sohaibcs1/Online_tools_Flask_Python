About Whois Lookup/Domain Lookup?

This WHOIS Lookup tool will show you who or what entity owns or controls that domain name, as well as their contact details like name, phone number, and address.

What is DNS?

The DNS, or domain name system, is a database of all internet addresses. The DNS system translates human-readable names into IP addresses, which are used to link people together.

How dns look up works?

The DNS lookup utility retrieves all of a domain's DNS records and arranges them into a prioritised list.It can extract data for the following ccTLD & TLD( E.g .club,.cn,.com,.game,.global,.in,.info and much more.)
